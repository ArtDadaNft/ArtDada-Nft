  • About our team

    Our team consists of two people. Dada, a girl passionate about drawing and art, is completing her high school art studies, and Fox, who is the programmer passionate about technology and art. Combining the qualities of one with those of the other they decided to create NFTs, and be curators of their own collection on opensea.io.

    Keep in touch: artdada.nft@gmail.com

    Our art

    Passion, graphite and good hands. Ours is an art that will last over time, it seeks compassion and undestranding in the viewer, bringing themes that concern everyday problems. Handrawn in graphite by Dada and modified by Fox.
